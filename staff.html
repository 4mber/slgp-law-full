Felina Rivera

Associate Attorney

Felina N. Rivera is a first generation attorney and 2013 graduate of the University of Oklahoma, College of Law. During law school, Ms. Rivera was the president of the Hispanic American Law Student Association (HALSA), and the founder and president of the organization Parents Attending Law School (PALS). She received multiple academic scholarships, including the Conner & Winters Diversity Scholarship, awarded to her for her excellent academic record and promising potential. Since obtaining her law degree, she has practiced in the areas of tax law, consumer protection, oil and gas, civil litigation and insurance defense. She has argued and won multiple motions for summary judgment and is proficient in legal research and writing.

Prior to law school, Ms. Rivera obtained a bachelor’s degree in journalism with emphasis in public relations from the University of Oklahoma in 2006. She had a successful career in marketing and PR, working as an account executive for a prominent advertising agency in Oklahoma City. She has also worked in the film and music industry. She presently performs as a singer-songwriter through Oklahoma and the Midwest region under the moniker, Felina & The Feels.

Ms. Rivera is native to the small town of Guymon, Oklahoma. Her father immigrated to the United States in the 1970s and her mother was a certified Oncology Nurse. Ms. Rivera has also been a long-time advocate for the legalization of hemp and marijuana for its many known uses, particularly for its use as pain management medication for those suffering from chronic illnesses. Ms. Rivera’s mother passed away in 2013 due to opioid use. Her passion for marijuana stems from this personal experience. This passion combined with her legal expertise in tax law and business formation make her an ideal attorney for the practice of cannabis law.
