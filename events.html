Journal Record Roundtable: Seed to Sell 2019
KOSU Studios, Oklahoma City, OK | June 27, 2019


The Journal Record held the first in a series of five roundtable discussions featuring thought leaders and early adapters on the cannabis and medical marijuana industry in Oklahoma. This discussion, “Seed to Sell,” focused on how this new industry is creating new business opportunities and economic growth for our state. Panelists include Ford Austin, president and CEO, APCO Media and APCO Medical; Sarah Lee Gossett Parrish, attorney at law; Zach Williams, founder, Go Go CannaBiz; and Keith Laird, Okie Kush Club. The discussion was moderated by Russell Ray, editor of The Journal Record.
