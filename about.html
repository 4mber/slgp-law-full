ABOUT

Sarah Lee Gossett Parrish

I am an experienced litigator, appellate attorney (state and federal appeals), oil and gas mineral contract negotiator, and insurance coverage counsel.

I am a professionally-trained, certified mediator, with civil, commercial, and employment mediation and negotiation training. I am licensed to practice in Oklahoma and Texas and am admitted to practice before the United States District Courts for the Western, Northern, and Eastern Districts of Oklahoma; the United States Court of Appeals for the Tenth Circuit; and the United States Supreme Court.

My articles have been published in The Federal Lawyer, The Oklahoma Bar Journal, and the American Bar Association’s Tort Trial & Insurance Practice Law Journal. I currently am a member of the ABA’s Litigation Section and its Appellate Practice Committee. I am a member of the Energy & Natural Resources Sections of the Oklahoma and Texas Bar Associations.

Click here to view my federal appellate practice article in the Oklahoma Bar Journal.

I also am a fine art photographer and have recently exhibited at Art San Diego 2016 and SPECTRUM Miami during Art Basel Miami. All images on this site were shot myself in Oklahoma City. I am represented by JRB Art at the Elms Gallery in Oklahoma City and Art4U Gallery in Parkville, just outside Kansas City. Please check out my fine art photography web site at slgparrish.com, which showcases my recent work, including my Flower Series, and my Instagram feed, @slgparrish, for more of my work.

My first novel, Guilt of Innocence, won second place in the New York Law Journal's Fiction for Lawyers contest in 2006, and one of my screenplays won the Best Little Pitchfest in Texas' THRILLER category in 2005.
